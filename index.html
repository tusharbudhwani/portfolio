ABOUT ME

As an individual, I have always believed in having conceptual clarity and strong fundamentals which helped me secure a GPA of 9.25/10. While the core courses in my undergraduate curriculum, such as Analysis of Algorithms, Operating Systems, Object Oriented Programming, and Computer Networks helped me build a perspective and straightforward approach to problem-solving, electives and capstone courses such as Blockchain and Cloud Computing helped me understand modern world problems. I enjoyed rendering my theoretical understanding into projects and learned how to create smart, sustainable, and scalable solutions using the latest tools and technologies.

Currently, for the Final year project, I am building a service with multiple predictive Machine Learning models bundled in a web-based application to accomplish early diagnosis of fatal diseases like heart strokes, breast cancer, etc. This project, being in the healthcare domain, helped me understand the importance of reliable training data and preprocessing to achieve maximum accuracy. I learned how to process the data using a variety of techniques, including the Chi-Square Test, and the Naive Random Oversampling Method.

To gain exposure to the software industry, I secured an internship as a Full Stack Developer at Adore India Pvt. Ltd. (Infovue Solutions) for a period of five months. There, I assisted in developing a Project Management System that helps in planning, tracking, and managing all the ongoing projects of the company, its sub-tasks, and the employees working on them. I heavily worked on PHP, JavaScript, Ajax, Bootstrap, and Git. Owing to my exceptional performance, I was given an opportunity to lead a team of interns for the subsequent project.

Besides academics, I actively participated in hackathons and coding competitions. One of my Hackathon projects named 'Codemate' finished in the top 10 out of over 120 participating teams. The project aimed at creating a collaboration and networking platform for coders and research enthusiasts where they can filter and find like-minded people based on their skill sets. Another project that I built along with my team in a hackathon was an online tool for managing household food and groceries. This online program tracks the expiry dates of food goods and assists in managing food concerns such as food spills and grocery management issues in the house to decrease food waste. Projects like these taught me how to create an impact in society using technology.

Apart from these co-curricular endeavors, I have also given extracurricular activities a profound inclination. In my third year of undergraduate, I served as a Technical Head of the National Service Scheme (NSS) at the University which is a community service association affiliated with the Government of India. I was involved in organizing over 80 social cause events during my tenure including Blood Donation Drives, Tree Plantation Drives, Teaching underprivileged children at small settlements/villages, Breast Cancer awareness campaigns, and many more. Through my participation in these endeavors, I not only contributed significantly to community service but also sharpened various skills, including leadership, time management, and communication.

I wish to work with leading tech companies and contribute to developing revolutionary software products. In the long run, I aspire to pursue entrepreneurship and design impactful software solutions for tomorrow's business needs.
